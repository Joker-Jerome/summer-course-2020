Overview

Join us to learn more about Topic 1 and Topic 2. The first few days will focus on the theory and applications of Topic 1. Many of our examples will build on each other throughout the course, so in order to get the most out of this, students will need to attend all courses and do all exercises. You will have opportunities to discuss the material and explore alternative strategies with the instructors and the TAs as the course progresses.
